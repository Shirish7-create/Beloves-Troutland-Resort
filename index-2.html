About Us

Beloves Troutland and Resort is a unique destination combining a peaceful resort experience with authentic Nepali trout fish farming. Located in the lush green hills of Kageshwori Manohara, Kathmandu, we offer comfortable accommodations, delicious local cuisine, and hands-on farm visits. Our guests enjoy fishing, nature walks, and the tranquility of rural Nepal.

  • Trout fish farming and guided tours
  • Traditional Nepali hospitality
  • Eco-friendly and family-friendly environment
  • Perfect for retreats, family outings, and nature lovers
